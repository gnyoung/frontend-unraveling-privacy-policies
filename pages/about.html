Privacy Grader is a Chrome extension that simplifies complex privacy policies. With clear summaries and warnings, it empowers users to make informed decisions about their data when signing up for web apps or services.

To use Privacy Grader, follow these simple steps:

  1. Navigate to the web page where the privacy policy is located.
  2. Click the Privacy Grader extension icon.
  3. Click "Scan" to produce a summary of the policy.
  4. You can also add a website to the "Avoid List" for warnings on revisits.
